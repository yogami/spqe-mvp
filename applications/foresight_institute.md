### Application Questions & Humanized Answers

**1. What is the core mission of your project?**
Our mission is to prevent the centralization of AI capabilities by building a verifiable, open-source trust layer for autonomous agents. Right now, if an AI agent makes a financial or data-driven decision, we just have to blindly trust the black-box model that generated it (like OpenAI or Anthropic). That is a terrible security model for Web3.

We built SemaProof to fix this. It is an offline, edge-capable classifier that intercepts the natural language outputs of AI agents and scores them for malicious intent. It acts like a Web Application Firewall, but for semantics rather than SQL injections. 

**2. How does your work align with the Foresight Institute's goals?**
We operate entirely in the "AI for Security" and "Private AI" spaces. By running our DistilBERT model locally and converting it to ONNX for sub-millisecond edge execution, we take the power of intent verification away from centralized tech monopolies and put it directly into the hands of the developers running the networks. We are building the exact type of decentralized oversight infrastructure that the Open Source AI community needs to stay safe.

**3. Why are you applying for this specific Node (Berlin)?**
We are based natively in Berlin right now. Our current operational base makes us a perfect fit for the new Berlin AI Safety Node launching on April 1st. We don't need relocation funding. What we need is non-dilutive capital to pay for the continuous fine-tuning of our open-source threat detection datasets, and we would deeply value the network and free compute provided by the physical incubator space.

**4. What are your milestones for the next 3 to 6 months?**
Our MVP is live and successfully intercepting semantic smuggling and zero-day prompt injections. For our next milestones, we are fully indexing against the EU AI Act and MITRE ATT&CK ATLAS framework to create the first legally-aligned, real-time threat detection index for on-chain AI agents. We will deliver a production-ready sidecar plugin (for Envoy/Kong) that any developer can drop into their stack in under 5 minutes.

**5. What is the background of the founding team?**
We specialize in deep-tech, cryptography, and Web3 security. We have built high-frequency trading execution engines and adversarial LLM test suites. We are focused purely on the intersection of deterministic, verifiable code and probabilistic AI outputs.
